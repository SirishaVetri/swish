Pricing Details

Pricing will be calculated per kilometer.

For ex: if you travel 100 kilometers your price will be 2 rs/ per kilometer.

100 * 2 rs/kilometer = 200 rs.

If you travel 200 kilometers or above your estimated cost will be 1.50 rs/Kilometer

300 * 1.5 rs/Kilometer = 450 rs.


Discounts

If you are a frequent traveller our automated system will give you discounts based on trips you travelled.

for ex: if you travel 10 trips automated system will give you 10% disocunt on your 11th to 20th trip.

If you are at 21st trip you'll recevice 20% discount.
