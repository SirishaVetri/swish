How can we help you?

Q. How to book a trip ?

A. Go to dashboard page fill in criteria and select a desired trip . You'll redirected to trip details page , click on Instant booking and you're all set for upcoming trip.

Q. What will be the price ?

A. Your price will be calculated based on how many kilometers you travelled.

Q. Do I get any discounts by using this website?

A. Yes , first time users will get 5% discounts and frequent travelers get discounts based on their number of trips

Q. How to know the pricing details for the trip?

A. For pricing please go to pricing page.
